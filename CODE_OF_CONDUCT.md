# Code of Conduct

## Our commitment

We are committed to a respectful, inclusive, and harassment-free community.

## Expected behavior

- Be respectful and constructive.
- Assume good intent.
- Focus feedback on code and ideas, not people.

## Unacceptable behavior

- Harassment, hate speech, or personal attacks
- Doxxing or sharing private information
- Repeated disruptive behavior

## Enforcement

Project maintainers may remove comments, close threads, or block contributors for violations.
